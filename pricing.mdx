**1 credit per second** of rendered video. 1 credit = $0.01 (100 credits per dollar).

Character and scene creation are free. A small credit hold is placed when you create characters to prevent abuse, but it's automatically refunded when your first render starts. Failed renders are also refunded.

**Concurrency** — Each organization can run up to 5 concurrent render jobs by default. Contact us if you need higher limits.

**Asset storage** — Stored characters and scenes never expire. Once created, they're available for reuse indefinitely.

To check your balance, use [Get Credit Balance](/api-reference/credits/balance).
