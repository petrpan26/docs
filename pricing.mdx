## How credits work

| Action | Credits | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| **Scene preprocessing** | 1/sec (held) | Refunded when first render starts |
| **Character preprocessing** | 1 (held) | Refunded when first render starts |
| **Render** | 1/sec | First render of preprocessed assets is free |
| **Failed render** | 0 | Fully refunded |

<Info>
  Preprocessing credits are **held** (not charged) until your first render. When you submit your first render using a preprocessed scene or character, the held credits are automatically refunded. This means preprocessing is effectively free as long as you render at least once.
</Info>
